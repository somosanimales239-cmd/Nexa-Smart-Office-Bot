Appointment communication regression verified
- The dedicated library contains 304 English/Spanish phrases, 34 intents and
  72 dynamic response templates.
- Active appointment context takes priority over general inventory Knowledge.
- The reported "algo mas disponible para ese dia" conversation is reproduced
  exactly and remains inside the appointment flow.
- Requested day and after-7 preference survive the complete conversation.
- The nearest next-day slot and a later exact preference match can be offered.
- The endpoint and dealer-appointment-availability:read scope are discovered.
- The dealer-agenda-calendar endpoint/read scope and appointment-create endpoint/write
  scope are discovered from every documented V6 connection-map field.
- Requests include from=YYYY-MM-DD and days=14.
- Weekly schedule, blocked/off dates, open dates, booked times and verified slots
  survive the safety filter while undocumented private fields do not.
- Live website availability is available to Knowledge and safe external AI context.
- A blocked/off date is never offered.
- The dealer's verified daily hours and same-day slot options are explained.
- An unavailable time produces verified same-day alternatives.
- A rejected set of times or blocked day advances to the next available day.
- An appointment decline leaves the dealer's verified contact information.
- A simple availability question cannot create an appointment.
- A short contextual selection can create only its exact verified slot.
- “sababdo en la mañana” resolves to Saturday, July 25 rather than Tuesday,
  July 21; “en la mañana” is never interpreted as tomorrow by itself.
- Prior dealer offer dates remain available as conversation context even when
  that day's slots disappear during refresh.
- Missing customer identity produces a name/contact request in the same chat,
  preserves the selected slot and does not end as an internal-only failure.
- After the customer supplies identity, the exact slot is revalidated, created
  and confirmed; if it changed, current verified alternatives are sent.
- "2 de la tarde" is parsed as 2:00 PM and a later contextual "1:30"
  resolves to the 1:30 PM option Nexa just offered.
- A time inquiry is distinct from an explicit booking commitment.
- Dealer/store/listing identifiers isolate schedules, blocked dates, slots and
  website appointments when reseller data contains more than one store.
- Another store's day off cannot erase the connected dealer's verified slots.
- An explicit day off for the same dealer remains authoritative.
- If an offered time disappears during refresh, Nexa offers the remaining
  current times without incorrectly declaring the whole date blocked.
- A booked or locally occupied time is never used for a new appointment.
- A booked website-calendar time is never offered or created.
- Website creation uses the exact V6 date/time/customer request body and requires
  appointment-create:write.
- After successful website creation the Dealer Agenda calendar is refreshed
  immediately and the local read cache is replaced.
- Authorized appointments are saved to the local Agenda when remote creation is
  unavailable or disabled.
